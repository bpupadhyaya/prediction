## Key Research Questions

1. What is the accuracy of existing natural disaster risk models for individual locations?
2. How much health risk is attributable to pollution exposure vs. other factors?
3. Which climate change impacts are most amenable to individual prediction?
4. How do FEMA flood maps compare to more recent flood risk models?
