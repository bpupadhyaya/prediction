## Full Description (Google Play — up to 4000 characters)

**Master the Market with 656 Parameters and On-Device AI**

Stock Prediction — AI Predictor is the most comprehensive, privacy-first stock market prediction tool on Android. Designed for serious investors and financial analysts, it combines a 656-parameter interactive prediction framework with an on-device AI research assistant and automated machine learning inference — all running entirely on your device.

---

**Interactive 656-Parameter Prediction Framework**

At the core of the app is a structured prediction model covering every major driver of stock market behavior, organized across six expert-crafted domains:

• Macro & Monetary Policy — Central bank rates, inflation, yield curves, money supply, and sovereign debt dynamics
• Fundamental Analysis — Earnings revisions, P/E ratios, revenue growth, balance sheet strength, and sector rotation
• Technical Analysis — Price momentum, moving averages, RSI, Bollinger Bands, volume trends, and chart patterns
• Sentiment & Behavioral — Retail surveys, institutional positioning, options skew, fear/greed indicators, and media tone
• Cross-Asset Signals — Currency strength, bond market signals, commodity trends, real estate indices, and intermarket correlations
• Geopolitical & Macro Risk — Trade policy uncertainty, energy supply risks, geopolitical conflict indices, and regulatory shifts

For each of the 656 parameters, set a directional weight (bullish, bearish, or neutral) and a confidence level. The app aggregates your inputs into a composite prediction score displayed as a live gauge at the top of the screen.

---

**On-Device AI Research Assistant**

Not sure what "yield curve inversion" means or how it historically impacts equities? Tap any parameter to open the AI Research panel. The built-in assistant — powered by Phi-3.5 Mini, Gemma 2, or Llama 3.2 — answers your questions with parameter-specific explanations, all running locally on your device.

No API keys. No subscriptions. No internet required once models are downloaded. Your questions and market data never leave your phone.

---

**Automated ML Prediction Engine**

The Model tab offers an automated GradientBoosting classifier compiled to ONNX format. Feed it your current parameter values and receive a direction forecast (Bullish / Bearish / Neutral) with a confidence score — computed entirely on-device.

---

**Plain-English and Technical Definitions**

Every one of the 656 parameters includes:
• Plain English: What the parameter means in everyday investor language
• Technical: The precise financial definition, formula, or academic interpretation

---

**Snapshot History and Export**

Save any prediction session as a named snapshot. Browse, compare, and export your history in multiple formats: JSON, CSV, PDF, or Excel. Track how your market views evolve over time.

---

**Privacy First — Zero Data Collection**

Everything runs on-device. Stock Prediction does not collect, transmit, or store any data. No analytics trackers, no third-party SDKs that phone home, no account required. Your investment research stays private.

Recommended: Android 8.0+ (API 26), 8 GB RAM for LLM features.
