## Description (App Store — up to 4000 characters)

**Master the Market with 656 Parameters and On-Device AI**

Stock Prediction — AI Predictor is the most comprehensive, privacy-first stock market prediction tool available on iOS. Designed for serious investors and financial analysts, it combines a 656-parameter interactive prediction framework with an on-device large language model (LLM) research assistant and automated ONNX machine learning inference — all running entirely on your device.

---

### Interactive 656-Parameter Prediction Framework

At the core of the app is a structured prediction model covering every major driver of stock market behavior. Parameters are organized across six expert-crafted domains:

- **Macro & Monetary Policy** — Central bank rates, inflation, yield curves, money supply, and sovereign debt dynamics
- **Fundamental Analysis** — Earnings revisions, P/E ratios, revenue growth, balance sheet strength, and sector rotation signals
- **Technical Analysis** — Price momentum, moving average crossovers, RSI, Bollinger Bands, volume trends, and chart pattern signals
- **Sentiment & Behavioral** — Retail sentiment surveys, institutional positioning, options market skew, fear/greed indicators, and media tone
- **Cross-Asset Signals** — Currency strength, bond market signals, commodity price trends, real estate indices, and intermarket correlations
- **Geopolitical & Macro Risk** — Trade policy uncertainty, energy supply risks, geopolitical conflict indices, and regulatory environment shifts

For each parameter, you can set a directional weight — bullish, bearish, or neutral — and assign a confidence level. The app aggregates your inputs into a composite prediction score displayed as a live gauge at the top of the screen.

---

### On-Device LLM Research Assistant

Not sure what "yield curve inversion" means or how it historically impacts equities? Tap any parameter to open the AI Research panel. The built-in LLM assistant — choose from Phi-3.5 Mini, Gemma 2, or Llama 3.2 — answers your questions with context-aware, parameter-specific explanations.

Because the LLM runs entirely on-device using Apple's neural engine and Metal GPU acceleration, your questions and market data never leave your iPhone or iPad. No API keys, no subscriptions, no internet connection required once models are downloaded.

---

### Automated ONNX Prediction Engine

The Model tab provides a second opinion: an automated GradientBoosting classifier compiled to ONNX format. Feed it your current parameter values and it returns a direction forecast (bullish/bearish/neutral) with a confidence score, computed entirely on-device with no external calls.

---

### Plain-English Parameter Definitions

Every one of the 656 parameters includes two descriptions:
- **Plain English**: What the parameter means, written for a general investor audience
- **Technical**: The precise financial definition, formula, or academic interpretation

---

### Snapshot History and Export

Save any prediction session as a named snapshot. The History tab lets you browse, compare, and export your past predictions in multiple formats: JSON, CSV, PDF, or Excel. Track how your market views evolve over time.

---

### Privacy First

Everything runs on-device. Stock Prediction does not collect, transmit, or store any data externally. There are no analytics trackers, no third-party SDKs that phone home, and no account required. Your investment research stays private.
