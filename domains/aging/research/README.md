## Key Research Questions

1. How early can dementia risk be reliably predicted using lifestyle factors alone?
2. What is the accuracy ceiling for fall prediction using self-report data?
3. How much variance in end-of-life costs is predictable vs. random?
4. What are the most impactful modifiable factors for healthy cognitive aging?
